How to Use

Welcome to our Flight Ticket Booking App! Follow the steps below to get started:

  1. Sign Up: If you are a new user, click on the "Sign Up" button to create an account. Fill in the required information and submit the form.
  2. Login: Once you have created an account, click on the "Login" button and enter your credentials (username and password) to log in to your account.
  3. Search Flights: After logging in, you will be redirected to the flight search page. Select your departure and destination airports, choose the departure date, and enter the number of passengers.
  4. View Available Flights: Click on the "Search" button to view the available flights for your search criteria. The results will display flight details such as flight number, duration, prices, and available seats.
  5. Book a Flight: Choose a flight from the list and click on the "Book" button to proceed with the booking. Follow the instructions to complete the booking process.
  6. Manage Bookings: You can manage your bookings by navigating to the "My Bookings" section. Here, you can view your booked flights, cancel bookings, and make changes if available.
  7. Logout: When you are finished using the application, click on the "Logout" button to securely log out of your account.
  8. Admin: There is also an admin login to add and remove flights.
    Admin Email: admin@admin.com
    Admin Password: admin
  9. Authentication: Separate authentication and validation for user and admin using Session Tokens

Enjoy using our Flight Ticket Booking App for all your travel needs!
